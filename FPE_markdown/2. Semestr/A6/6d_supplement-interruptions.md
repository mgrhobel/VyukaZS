**A6 SUPPLEMENT**

**Interruptions --** ***face2face*** **Unit 6D**

\

***Asking permission to interrupt***

\

**Text**

\

1.  Sorry to bother you, but have you got a minute/do you have a minute?

2.  Is this a good time?

3.  Sorry to disturb you.

4.  I was wondering if I could see you/talk to you for a moment.

5.  Are you busy?

6.  Can I have a word?

\

**Extra possibilities**

\

1.  Excuse me, do you have/have you got a minute?

2.  Excuse me, I (just) need a minute (of your time).

3.  Excuse me, I (just) need a minute, if you have time.

4.  Excuse me, I just need a minute to ask you a question/to tell you
    something about...../

to pass on some information about...../ to give you some important
information.

5.  Sorry, but can/may I interrupt for a minute?

6.  Sorry to butt in, but do you have a sec? (very informal)

7.  I know you're busy (now), but can you (just) give me a minute/a few
    minutes?

8.  I'm really sorry, but I absolutely need to talk to you right
    away/straight away. (very urgent)

9.  Excuse me, can I (just) ask a quick question?

10. Got a minute? (informal)

\

***Refusing permission to interrupt***

\

**Text**

\

1.  Sorry, this (really) isn't a good time.

2.  (Sorry,) I'm really up against it at the moment.

3.  (Sorry,) I'm afraid I'm a bit tied up just now.

4.  (Sorry,) I'm rather pushed for time at the moment.

5.  (Sorry,) I'm really rather busy right now.

\

**Extra possibilities**

\

1.  Sorry, but I can't talk right now. Can you come back in an
    hour/later?

2.  Sorry, I don't have time just/right now.

3.  Sorry, but I've got to finish (this report, etc.) first./I've got an
    important

appointment in a few minutes./ I have to run./ I need to make

a call first./I'm just leaving.

4\. No, really, this is not a good time. Catch me later. (rather
informal)

5\. Well, no, actually. I haven't even got a second right now. Try me
later.

\

\

\

\

\

\

***If refused***, it's common to make some little comment or apology.

\

**Text**

\

Don't worry, it's not (that) important./it can wait./it's not urgent.

OK, I'll catch you later/some other time.

(Right,) I'll come back later/call you later.

\

**Extra possibilities**

\

OK, sorry**.** I'll catch you later./I'll ask you later./I'll talk to
you later.

OK, I'll catch up with you later./give you a call later/come by later.

\

***Giving permission to interrupt***

\

**Text** (p. 127)

\

1.  Yes, of course, what can I do for you?

2.  Yes, of course, how can I help?

3.  Sure, what's the problem?

4.  What's up? (informal)

\

**Extra possibilities**

\

1.  Yeah, sure. What's up? (very informal)

2.  Certainly, how can I help you? (very formal)

3.  Yes, sure. Is there a problem?

4.  No problem. What's up? (informal)

5.  OK, come on in./Let's find a place to talk./Have a seat.

6\. OK, hang on. I'll be right with you./ Hang on a sec, I'll be right
with you.
